Cultural Sites

Kenya's cultural heritage is a rich blend of traditions, languages, and customs from over 40 ethnic groups, including the Kikuyu, Luo, Luhya, Maasai, and Swahili. This diversity is reflected in Kenya’s music, dance, cuisine, clothing, and festivals.
